paspel

Pastel

Paspel is like a dessert and it is important because it is something that cannot be missed on a birthday.

Ingredients

  • arina
  • huevos
  • Water
  • strawberry
  • leche
  • vannia

Method

  1. Put the arina en un plato ancho y grande
  2. Then you make a hole in the middle of the flour, add 4 or 5 eggs and stir it well.
  3. Afterwards, you put the dough on a plate to put it in the oven and leave it for 30 or 45 minutes.
  4. Then you made vain around the dough.
  5. If you want you can put fruntra with strawberry and melon, banana.
